User Guide

Step 1: Open the Website

Start by opening the HealthPulse website in your browser.

Step 2: Upload Your Report

Click on the Choose File button and select your health report in PDF format. Ensure your report is properly compressed.

Step 3: Verify Extracted Values

Review the extracted test values and also make sure that they are in the same units as mentioned. If any values are incorrect, you can edit them directly.

Step 4: View Your Health Advice

View your personalized health insights and actionable recommendations based on your test results.

Step 5: Explore Blogs

Share your health journey or read tips and advice from others in our community blog section. Engage with comments and replies to connect and learn!
